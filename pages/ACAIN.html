ACAIN

The Association of Children's Authors and Illustrators of Nigeria (ACAIN) is a professional organization dedicated to promoting and supporting the work of authors and illustrators in the field of children's literature in Nigeria.

Role

As a UX/UI designer, my role in the Association of Children's Authors and Illustrators of Nigeria (ACAIN) project was to create a visually appealing and user-friendly website that enhances the user experience for both authors and illustrators, promotes their work, and provides a platform for collaboration and networking.


Team

I collaborated closely with a multidisciplinary team consisting of ACAIN stakeholders, including authors, illustrators, and members of the ACAIN executive board. The team also included developers, content creators, and project managers who were responsible for executing the project.


The Problem

ACAIN faced several challenges in showcasing the work of its members and facilitating meaningful connections between authors and illustrators. The existing website lacked a cohesive design, making it difficult for users to navigate and find relevant information. The user experience was disjointed and did not effectively engage visitors, resulting in missed opportunities for member growth and collaboration.

The User Experience

The redesigned website aimed to provide a seamless and engaging experience for users. Key considerations for the user experience included:
Intuitive Navigation: Implementing a clear and user-friendly navigation structure to allow users to easily explore the site and find relevant information, such as member profiles, upcoming events, and resources.

Visual Appeal: Creating a visually appealing design that reflects the vibrancy and creativity of children's literature and illustrations in Nigeria. Using a harmonious color scheme, engaging imagery, and typography to captivate users and convey a sense of professionalism.

Member Profiles: Developing a comprehensive member profile system that allows authors and illustrators to showcase their work, highlight their achievements, and connect with other members for collaboration opportunities.

Collaboration Tools: Incorporating features such as forums, messaging, and project collaboration spaces to facilitate networking and collaboration among members.

The Solution

To address the challenges faced by ACAIN, the proposed solution was to redesign the website with a focus on enhancing the user experience. This involved creating an intuitive and visually appealing interface, improving content organization, and implementing features that promote collaboration and networking among authors and illustrators.

Process

The UX/UI design process for ACAIN's website involved the following stages:
Research: Conducting user research and stakeholder interviews to understand the needs, pain points, and goals of ACAIN members. Analyzing competitor websites and industry best practices to gather insights and identify opportunities.
Wireframing and Prototyping: Creating low-fidelity wireframes to define the website's structure, layout, and content organization. Iteratively refining these wireframes based on user feedback and stakeholder input. Developing interactive prototypes to validate the proposed user flows and gather further feedback.
Visual Design: Crafting a visual design that aligned with ACAIN's brand identity and resonated with the target audience. Creating a style guide to ensure consistency across the website's interface elements, including typography, color palette, and imagery.

Results

The redesigned ACAIN website successfully transformed the user experience, leading to several positive outcomes:
Improved Engagement: Users found the website more visually appealing and engaging, resulting in increased time spent on the site and reduced bounce rates. This led to a higher likelihood of discovering new authors and illustrators.
Enhanced Networking: Authors and illustrators benefited from the member profile system, which allowed them to showcase their work to a wider audience and connect with other professionals in their field. This resulted in increased collaboration opportunities and a stronger sense of community within ACAIN.
Streamlined Information Access: The intuitive navigation and improved content organization made it easier for users to find relevant information quickly. This included details about upcoming events, workshops, industry news, and resources, enhancing the overall user experience.
